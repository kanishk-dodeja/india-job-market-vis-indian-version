Disclaimer

Last updated: 10 June 2026

This dashboard is for education and exploration. It is not official data, professional advice, or a prediction engine.

Modeled Estimates

Worker counts, pay, outlook, education, and AI exposure values are modeled estimates built from public source anchors. They are intended for comparison across occupation groups, not exact measurement.

No Career, Financial, Legal, or Policy Advice

The dashboard should not be used as the sole basis for career planning, hiring decisions, financial decisions, academic conclusions, public policy decisions, or legal advice. Verify important decisions with primary sources and qualified experts.

AI Exposure Is Not Job Loss

AI exposure means that some tasks in an occupation may be technically exposed to automation or augmentation. It does not mean that the occupation will disappear, that workers will lose jobs, or that future demand is guaranteed to fall.

Source and Timeliness Limits

Public reports can be revised, moved, superseded, or interpreted differently. The project may be updated as better data becomes available.

Student and Public Use

Students, educators, and general readers may use the visualizer as a starting point for discussion. It should be cited as a source-backed modeled visualization, not as an official government table.
