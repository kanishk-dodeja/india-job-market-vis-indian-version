# India Job Market Visualizer

An India-focused interactive treemap of occupations, worker counts, median pay, employment outlook, education level, and digital AI exposure.

Live site: https://kanishk-dodeja.github.io/india-job-market-vis-indian-version/

## Source Standard

This copy uses only publicly inspectable, credible source references in the public documentation and footer:

- PLFS 2022-23, NSO/MoSPI
- NCO-2015, Directorate General of Employment, Ministry of Labour and Employment
- ILO Working Paper 96, Generative AI and Jobs (2023)
- ILO Working Paper 140, refined occupational GenAI exposure index (2025)
- WEF Future of Jobs Report 2023
- India Employment Report 2024, IHD and ILO
- RBI Annual Report 2022-23
- NASSCOM Strategic Review 2025
- McKinsey future-of-work research

See `scoring-notes.md` for methodology and caveats, and `DATA_CONFIDENCE_AUDIT.md` for the 95% confidence standard used in this hosted version.

## Important Caveat

This is a modeled explanatory dashboard. It is not an official government dataset and should not be read as a precise job-loss forecast. AI exposure means task exposure to current AI systems; it does not automatically mean worker displacement.
